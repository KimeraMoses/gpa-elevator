About Us

Welcome to GPA ELEVATOR a one stop website for all university/college academic materials.This provides students of higher instutions with online access to past papers and other study materials in a softcopy format. GPA ELEVATOR is under development being managed by two Makerere University students of computer engineering by names of KIMERA MOSES and MUBIRU ISAAC with an aim of simplifying the lives of university students by bringing important academic materials like past-papers, lecture Notes and much more closer to them.
To fully enjoy the exciting features of Gpa elevator,sign in to create your own account that will enable you recieve information that suit your interests.If you already have an account please click the login button to get started with the amazing features of the website. Once more your are Welcome to the center of excellence.


MISSION


Our mission is to create a connection between students of different universities and simplify their life by bringing important academic materials like pastpapers,lecture Notes,links to important textbooks closer to them

VISION


To create a reliable, effecient, affordable and environmentally friendly way of flow of information and academic materials for all the universities in uganda.
